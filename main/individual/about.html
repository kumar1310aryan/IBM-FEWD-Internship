About MealShare


At MealShare, our mission is to reduce food waste and alleviate hunger through effective food redistribution. We connect surplus food from restaurants, hotels, and individuals with NGOs that serve those in need. By facilitating these connections, we strive to make our communities more sustainable and compassionate.

Our Mission


Our mission is to provide a sustainable solution to food waste by bridging the gap between surplus food sources and organizations that can put it to good use. We believe that no food should go to waste when it can be used to nourish those in need.

Our Impact


Since our inception, MealShare has made significant strides in reducing food waste and supporting communities.
  • Meals Saved: Over 50,000 meals redistributed
  • Food Donors: Partnered with 200+ restaurants and hotels
  • NGOs Supported: 150+ local organizations
