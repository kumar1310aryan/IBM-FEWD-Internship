Share More,
Waste Less

MealShare is dedicated to creating a world where no food goes to waste and everyone has access to nutritious meals. Our platform bridges the gap between those who have excess food and those who need it most, including various non-profit organizations and local communities. By fostering a network of responsible food donors and committed recipients, we aim to build a more sustainable and compassionate society.
HOW IT WORKS

Register and Share Your Food

Sign up on our website and provide details about the surplus food you have available. Whether you’re a restaurant, hotel, or an individual with extra food, let us know what you have to offer.

Schedule a Pickup


Once you’ve registered, our network of non-profit and government organizations will arrange for delivery personnel to pick up the food from your location at a convenient time.

Food Redistribution


The collected food is then delivered to local NGOs and community organizations that serve those in need, ensuring that your surplus food helps nourish individuals and families in your community.
